Jangligonda Village

Jangligonda is a village in Mahabubabad tehsil of Warangal district, Telangana. It is located about 14 km from Mahabubabad tehsil headquarters and around 75 km from Warangal district headquarters. Jangligonda is the Gram Panchayat for Jangligonda village.
